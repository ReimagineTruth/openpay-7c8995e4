# OpenPay Feature Guide

OpenPay is a Pi-powered wallet and merchant payment stack designed for fast checkout, transparent records, and modern security. This guide walks through every major feature, grouped for quick scanning and deeper exploration.

OpenPay works in the Pi Browser. Sign in securely with Pi authentication at https://openpy.space/auth to enjoy full-screen experience, notifications, POS, Merchant Portal access, and more.

## Core Wallet Features

- Express Send and Transfer — Send Pi instantly and move balance quickly for everyday use.
- QR Receive and Request Payment — Get paid in-person with QR or remotely via payment requests/links.
- Invoices and Receipts — Send invoices, track paid status, and keep clean records.
- Activity and OpenLedger — Transparent payment history with auditable receipt details.
- Currency Converter — Clear value conversions with a stable in-app Pi reference.
- Security and Disputes — Pi-auth sign-in, device controls, and dispute tools.

### Express Send and Transfer
Move funds quickly with minimal steps. Express Send streamlines the payment flow so day-to-day transfers feel instant and reliable. Designed for personal and small-business use where speed matters and records remain clear.
Try it: https://openpy.space/auth/send

### QR Receive and Request Payment
Accept payments in-person using a scannable QR or request payment remotely with a link. Both flows generate clean confirmations and receipts, making counter sales and remote invoices simple to manage.
Try it: https://openpy.space/auth/receive and https://openpy.space/auth/request-payment

### Invoices and Receipts
Create professional invoices, track status, and issue receipts automatically upon payment. Keep clear records for customers and internal accounting, with auditable details stored in your activity history.
Try it: https://openpy.space/auth/send-invoice

### Activity and OpenLedger
Review transparent payment history with searchable entries and receipt detail. OpenLedger provides a public view for eligible transactions, supporting trust, audits, and dispute resolution.
Explore: https://openpy.space/auth/activity and https://openpy.space/auth/ledger

### Currency Converter
Display values in your preferred currency while keeping an in-app Pi reference for consistency. Conversions help buyers and sellers communicate value clearly, especially across regions and use cases.
Try it: https://openpy.space/auth/currency-converter

### Security and Disputes
Sign in with Pi-auth, use device and PIN safeguards, and access dispute tools when something goes wrong. The security model prioritizes identity assurance and transparent records to resolve issues fairly.
Explore: https://openpy.space/auth/disputes and https://openpy.space/auth/confirm-pin
